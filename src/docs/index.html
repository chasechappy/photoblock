PhotoBlock Preview

PhotoBlock is a secure, visual account and password manager that uses photos and emojis for blockchain and website authentication. You provide a photo and select a sequence of emojis called an EmojiKey ... PhotoBlock does the rest.

Photo + Emojis = PhotoBlock

PhotoBlock can be used by anybody, but is designed primarily for non-technical users.

If you'd like to compare it to something, imagine a different kind of Two-factor Authentication (2FA) where a sequence of emojis is your password ("the thing you know"), and a photo of your choice is your device ("the thing you have"). PhotoBlock must first be added to an app or website before it is available to end-users.

The icon above and the stuff that happens when you select it, is PhotoBlock.

Everything else on this page exists to give you an idea of how PhotoBlock works when embedded into an app or website created by other developers.
